Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  2. Mix brown and granulated sugar with melted butter in bowl.
  3. Add egg and vanilla extract to sugar and butter mixture. Mix until combined.
  4. Combine backing soda and salt in second bowl.
  5. Add bowl of dry ingredients to wet ingredients and combine.
  6. Slowly add flour to mixture. Mix until smooth.
  7. Once smooth, add in chocolate chips.
  8. Roll batter into small balls and place onto a baking sheet.
  9. Bake in oven for 11-14 minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
